I'm Thriwax
About Me

Thrinax + Wax = Thriwax

Thriwax is a multi-genre ambient artist, but for us he recorded a mix of broken rhythms interspersed with hypnotic sound
structures, breaks and a dead drum.

He started his project in 2018 with the presentation of the album in the clubs "NII" and "Club",
and also played in Kazan's Werk and other clubs.

In his work, he is inspired by the metamorphoses of the weather.
